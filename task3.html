Indian Recipe: The Great Indian Thali

Ingredients

  • wheat bread
  • minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, saut√© the onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and garnish with Parmesan cheese.

Quick Facts

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 80 minutes
Servings: 4
