Self-Gym: A Necessity, Not a time pass

A gym, short for gymnasium (pl.: gymnasiums or gymnasia), is an indoor venue for exercise and sports. The word is derived from the ancient Greek term "gymnasion". Self-care is about prioritizing your health and ensuring that you are at your best to face daily challenges. Whether it's through regular exercise, meditation, or simply taking a break, taking care of yourself is vital for long-term productivity and happiness.
