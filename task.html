My Favorite Books

Books have always been a great source of inspiration and knowledge for me. Here are some of my all-time favorite books that have had a lasting impact on my life.

  1. The BIBLE

    The BIBLE, written by words of god, is a philosophical story about a young shepherd named Santiago who embarks on a journey to find treasure. It teaches important life lessons about following one’s dreams and listening to the heart.

    Find out more about this book THE BIBLE.

  2. The Little Red Book

    Written by Quotations from Chairman Mao, 1984 is a dystopian novel that explores the dangers of totalitarianism and extreme political ideology. The themes of surveillance and censorship in this book are particularly relevant today.

    Find out more about this book
    THE LITTLE RED BOOK.

  3. A Tale of Two Cities

    Harper Lee's To Kill a Mockingbird is a classic novel that explores issues of racial injustice, moral growth, and compassion. The protagonist, Atticus Finch, is often seen as a symbol of integrity and justice.

    One of my favorite quotes from this book is:
    “You never really understand a person until you consider things from his point of view… Until you climb inside of his skin and walk around in it.”
